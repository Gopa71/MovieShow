ONE PIECE

One Piece is a Japanese anime television series produced by Toei Animation12. It premiered on Fuji TV in October 1999 and is based on Eiichiro Oda's manga series of the same name1. The story follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a boy whose body gained the properties of rubber after unintentionally eating a Devil Fruit1. The series has been broadcast in over 170 countries worldwide, making it one of the most successful anime franchises in history2
